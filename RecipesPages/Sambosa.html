Sambosa

A quintessential crispy appetizer: golden fried pastry pockets filled with savory spiced meat.

PREP. TIME:

30 Mins

COOK. TIME:

10 Mins

SERVINGS:

24 Pieces

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Sambosa wrappers
  • 500g Ground beef
  • 1 large Onion (finely diced)
  • 1/2 cup Fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp Allspice (Baharat)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 tbsp Flour + 2 tbsp Water

Preparation:

1

The Filling: Sauté diced onions until soft, add ground beef and cook until browned, then stir in spices and parsley. Let cool.

2

The Glue: Mix the flour and water into a thick paste to use for sealing the pastry edges.

3

Folding: Place a teaspoon of filling at the end of a pastry strip and fold diagonally to form a triangle.

4

Zig-Zag: Continue folding the triangle along the strip in a zig-zag motion until you reach the end.

5

Sealing: Dab a small amount of the flour paste on the remaining edge and tuck it into the fold to seal tightly.

6

Frying: Heat oil in a deep pan and fry sambosa in batches for 2-3 minutes until golden brown and bubbly.
