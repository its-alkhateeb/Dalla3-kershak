Lava Cake

A decadent, melt-in-your-mouth chocolate cake with a warm molten center, served with a cold scoop of vanilla ice cream.

PREP. TIME:

10 Mins

COOK. TIME:

12 Mins

SERVINGS:

2 People

Ingredients:

  • 100g High-quality dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted butter
  • 2 large Eggs + 2 Egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup Granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp All-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 2 scoops Vanilla ice cream

Preparation:

1

Melt: Melt the dark chocolate and butter together in a heat-proof bowl over simmering water (or in the microwave) until smooth.

2

Whisk: In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, yolks, sugar, and vanilla until thick and pale. Fold in the melted chocolate.

3

Combine: Gently sift the flour and salt into the mixture. Fold until just combined—do not overmix.

4

Bake: Pour the batter into greased and floured ramekins. Bake at 200°C for 10-12 minutes until the edges are firm but the center is soft.

5

The Flip: Let the cakes cool for 1 minute, then carefully run a knife around the edge and invert them onto a plate.

6

The Topping: Immediately place a large scoop of cold vanilla ice cream on top of the hot cake and serve instantly.
