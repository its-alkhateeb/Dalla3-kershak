Cheese Burger

from the juiciest burger patties to the best buns and burger sauces.

PREP. TIME:

30 Mins

COOK. TIME:

10 Mins

SERVINGS:

6 Burgers

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb ground chuck beef, (80/20)
  • freshly ground black pepper and sea salt, to taste
  • 6 burger buns
  • 6 slices medium cheddar cheese, (thick sliced)
  • 1 large tomato, sliced into 8 rings
  • 1/2 medium red onion, thinly sliced into rings
  • 1/3 cup mayo + 1 tsp BBQ Sauce

Preparation:

1

Prepare Toppings: Slice and prepare all of your burger toppings and sauces.

2

Toast Buns: Spread the cut-side of the bun with softened or melted butter and toast the cut side on a skillet or on the grill until golden brown.

3

Prepare Beef Patties: Preheat grill to medium heat. Divide ground beef into 6 portions (1/3 lb each) and form patties that are about 1/2" thick and 1" wider than the burger buns. Season patties generously with salt and pepper.

4

Grill hamburger patties: (indent-side-up) covered for 3-5 min each side on the grill. Flip burgers when juices are accumulating on top of the burger and you have a good sear.

5

Adding Cheese: Apply cheese in the last 1-2 minutes of grilling then cover with the lid and cook until 160˚F

6

BBQ Aioli: Mix 1/3 cup mayo + 1 tsp BBQ Sauce
