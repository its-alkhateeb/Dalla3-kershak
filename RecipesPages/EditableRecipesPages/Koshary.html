Egyptian Koshary

A traditional vegan street food staple consisting of rice, lentils, and pasta.

PREP. TIME:

20 Mins

COOK. TIME:

40 Mins

SERVINGS:

4 People

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Brown Lentils
  • 1 cup White Rice
  • 1 cup Elbow Macaroni
  • 2 cups Tomato Sauce
  • 2 White Onions (sliced)
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tsp Cumin & Chili

Preparation:

1

Base: Cook the lentils and rice in salted water until tender.

2

Pasta: Boil macaroni separately until al dente.

3

Sauce: Sauté garlic with cumin, add tomato sauce, and simmer.

4

Onions: Fry onion slices in oil until dark brown and crispy.

5

Plating: Layer rice/lentils, then pasta, then sauce, and top with onions.
