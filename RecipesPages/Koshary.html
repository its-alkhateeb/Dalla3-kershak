Egyptian Koshary

A traditional vegan street food staple consisting of rice, lentils, and pasta.

⏱ Prep Time: 20 Mins
🔥 Cook Time: 40 Mins
🍽 Servings: 4 People
🥗 Category: Main / Vegan

Description

Koshary is a hearty, multi-layered dish. It combines brown lentils and rice with macaroni, topped with a spicy tomato sauce (Salsa) and garnished with chickpeas and crispy fried onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Brown Lentils
  • 1 cup White Rice
  • 1 cup Elbow Macaroni
  • 2 cups Tomato Sauce
  • 2 White Onions (sliced)
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tsp Cumin & Chili

Instructions

  1. Base: Cook the lentils and rice in salted water until tender.
  2. Pasta: Boil macaroni separately until al dente.
  3. Sauce: Sauté garlic with cumin, add tomato sauce, and simmer.
  4. Onions: Fry onion slices in oil until dark brown and crispy.
  5. Plating: Layer rice/lentils, then pasta, then sauce, and top with onions.

Technical Notes

Storage: Components are best stored separately in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Substitutions: Use brown rice for a higher fiber alternative.
