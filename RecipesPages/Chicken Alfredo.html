Creamy Chicken Alfredo

A rich and velvety Italian-American classic featuring tender grilled chicken and silky parmesan cream sauce.

⏱ Prep Time: 10 Mins
🔥 Cook Time: 20 Mins
🍽 Servings: 2 People
🥗 Category: Main / Italian

Description

A classic Italian-American comfort dish featuring tender grilled chicken breast and fettuccine pasta tossed in a rich, velvety sauce made of parmesan cheese, heavy cream, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 250g Fettuccine pasta
  • 2 large Chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Pasta: Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the fettuccine until "al dente" (firm to the bite). Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Chicken: Season chicken with salt and pepper. In a large pan, melt 1 tbsp of butter and sear chicken over medium-high heat until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauce: In the same pan, melt the remaining butter. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Emulsify: Stir in the Parmesan cheese slowly until melted. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water.
  5. Combine: Toss the cooked pasta and chicken into the sauce. Stir until every strand is coated. Top with parsley.

Technical Notes

Pro-Tip: Never rinse your pasta after boiling! The starch on the surface of the pasta helps the Alfredo sauce "stick" to the noodles.. Serving: This dish is best served immediately. If reheating, add a splash of milk to the pan to loosen the sauce, as the cream will thicken significantly in the fridge.
